Acrylic

Amalia wanted to learn more about art than what taught at school so she joined an external club when she was 6 years old and only left once she graduated high school.

Textile

During the pandemic Amalia started drawing on her own clothes to upcicle them. Later on, she bought textile markers and created a collection of tshirts for her friends.

Charcoal

Thanks to her Figure Drawing class at Drexel University, Amalia decided to pursue a minor in Fine Arts. First time ever using charcoal, she fell in love with it.
