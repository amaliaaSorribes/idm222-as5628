About

I am a senior student at Drexel University with an expected graduation date of September 2025. I am on track to complete a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, along with two minors: User Experience and Interaction Design, and Fine Arts.

As an international student from Spain, Philadelphia has provided me with valuable opportunities to enhance my professional skills.

During my time in college, while taking classes, I have worked as a Teacher Assistant at Drexel for the College of Computing and Informatics. In total, I have been employed to help students with 5 different courses: CI103- Computing and Informatics Design III, CS164- Introduction to Computer Science, CS171- Computer Programming I, CS281- Systems Architecture, and SE201- Introduction to Software Engineering.

I have also previously worked as a teacher and teacher assistant for the technological summer camp of ICAI University.

I recently worked 6 months as a digital experience & marketing intern at Reading Terminal Market, one of America's largest and oldest public markets. I kept the website updated daily with new events and notifications, created a new interactive visualization of the market map to improve navigation, and created many designs for event promotion. I learned a lot about diverse fields in a never-stopping public space, at a time of preparation for a 5 year strategic plan publication.

Previously, I worked as a front-end website developer intern for 6 months at Framewrk agency, a startup whose goal is to help entrepreneurs grow their business. I had the opportunity to learn how a project develops in a professional background and to help create new features on the website. The project I am most proud of is making a discussion forums chat card, that enabled entrepreneurs to gain information about different topics. As part of this, I had to dive into the backend to store and retrieve data. During that time, I also became extremely attracted to UX design and decided to pursue a minor in it.

I am always working towards learning and gaining more experience in this field, and towards building this creative portfolio to output my efforts and achievements. I have always been hugely interested in art and love to paint and draw, so I multiple art class electives. Consequently, I added a minor in Fine Arts to my studies, and I am now excited to keep on constructing my portfolio with a mix of both artistic capabilities and technological skill.
